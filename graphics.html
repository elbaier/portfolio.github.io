GRAPHICS

This series of digital artworks was a challenge to create an original figure that could be colored using four different color schemes.

I thought quite a lot about how rigidness and movement, music and dance, and hardness and softness could come together, and ended up creating this ballerina-skater figure.

My professor said that, in the end, my figure reminded him of Edith Piaf. If you want the full experience, listen to "Mon Dieu" or "La Vie en Rose" while gazing at these.
